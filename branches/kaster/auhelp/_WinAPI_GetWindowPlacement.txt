
_WinAPI_GetWindowPlacement

Retrieves the placement of the window for Min, Max, and normal positions

#Include 
_WinAPI_GetWindowPlacement($hWnd)

---  Таблица параметров  ---
$hWnd
	Handle of the window

---  Таблица возвращаемых значений  ---
Успех:	$tagWINDOWPLACEMENT structure with the placement coordinates
Ошибка:	0, @error = 1, @extended = _WinAPI_GetLastError()

---  Смотрите также  ---
_WinAPI_SetWindowPlacement, $tagWINDOWPLACEMENT
