
_GUIToolTip_DelTool

Removes a tool

#Include 
_GUIToolTip_DelTool($hWnd, $hTool[, $iID = 0])

---  Таблица параметров  ---
$hWnd
	Handle to control
$hTool
	Handle to the window that contains the tool
$iID
	[опционально] Identifier of the tool
