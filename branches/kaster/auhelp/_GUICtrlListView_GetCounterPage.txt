
_GUICtrlListView_GetCounterPage

Calculates the number of items that can fit vertically in the visible area

#Include 
_GUICtrlListView_GetCounterPage($hWnd)

---  Таблица параметров  ---
$hWnd
	Handle to the control

---  Таблица возвращаемых значений  ---
Return the number of fully visible items

---  Примечания  ---
If the current view is icon or small icon view, the return value is the total number
of items in the list-view control.
