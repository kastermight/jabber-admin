
_WinAPI_GetAsyncKeyState

Determines whether a key is up or down at the time the function is called

#Include 
_WinAPI_GetAsyncKeyState($iKey)

---  Таблица параметров  ---
$iKey
	Key to test for

---  Таблица возвращаемых значений  ---
Успех:	If the most significant bit is set the key is down, and if the least significant bit is set,
	the key was pressed after the previous call to GetAsyncKeyState.
Ошибка:	0, if a window in another thread or process currently has the keyboard focus.
