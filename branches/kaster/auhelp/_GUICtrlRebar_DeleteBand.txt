
_GUICtrlRebar_DeleteBand

Deletes a band from a rebar control

#Include 
_GUICtrlRebar_DeleteBand($hWnd, $iIndex)

---  Таблица параметров  ---
$hWnd
	Handle to rebar control
$iIndex
	Zero-based index of the band to be deleted

---  Таблица возвращаемых значений  ---
Успех:	True
Ошибка:	False
