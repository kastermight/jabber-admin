
_GDIPlus_BrushGetSolidColor

Get the color of a Solid Brush object

#Include 
_GDIPlus_BrushGetSolidColor($hBrush, [$iARGB = 0xFF000000])

---  Таблица параметров  ---
$hBrush
	Handle to a Brush object
$iARGB
	[опционально] The color of the brush.

---  Таблица возвращаемых значений  ---
Успех:	Brush color
Ошибка:	-1 and @error is set
