
_GUICtrlHeader_GetBitmapMargin

Retrieves the width of the bitmap margin

#Include 
_GUICtrlHeader_GetBitmapMargin($hWnd)

---  Таблица параметров  ---
$hWnd
	Handle to control

---  Таблица возвращаемых значений  ---
Успех: The width of the bitmap margin in pixels. If the bitmap margin was not previously specified,
		the default value of 3 * GetSystemMetrics(SM_CXEDGE) is returned.
Ошибка: 0
