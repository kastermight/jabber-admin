
_GUICtrlMonthCal_GetUnicodeFormat

Retrieves the Unicode character format flag for the control

#Include 
_GUICtrlMonthCal_GetUnicodeFormat($hWnd)

---  Таблица параметров  ---
$hWnd
	Handle to control

---  Таблица возвращаемых значений  ---
True: Control is using Unicode characters
False: Control is using ANSI characters
