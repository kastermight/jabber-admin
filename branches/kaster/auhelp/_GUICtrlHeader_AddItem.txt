
_GUICtrlHeader_AddItem

Adds a new header item

#Include 
_GUICtrlHeader_AddItem($hWnd, $sText[, $iWidth = 50[, $iAlign = 0[, $iImage = -1[, $fOnRight = False]]]])

---  Таблица параметров  ---
$hWnd
	Handle to control
$sText
	Item text
$iWidth
	[опционально] Item width
$iAlign
	[опционально] Text alignment:
		0 - Text is left-aligned
		1 - Text is right-aligned
		2 - Text is centered
$iImage
	[опционально] Zero based index of an image within the image list
$fOnRight
	[опционально] If True, the column image appears to the right of text

---  Таблица возвращаемых значений  ---
Успех:	The index of the new item
Ошибка:	-1

_GUICtrlHeader_InsertItem, _GUICtrlHeader_DeleteItem
