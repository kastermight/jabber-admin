
_GUICtrlTab_FindTab

Searches for a tab with the specific text

#Include 
_GUICtrlTab_FindTab($hWnd, $sText[, $fInStr = False[, $iStart = 0]])

---  Таблица параметров  ---
$hWnd
	Handle to the control
$sText
	Text to search for
$fInStr
	[опционально] If True, the text can be anywhere in the tab's text.
$iStart
	[опционально] Zero based index of the tab to start searching from

---  Таблица возвращаемых значений  ---
Успех:	The zero based tab index that matches the search
Ошибка:	-1

---  Примечания  ---
The search is case insensitive
