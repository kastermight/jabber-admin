
_GUICtrlMonthCal_GetMaxSelCount

Retrieves the maximum date range that can be selected in a month calendar control

#Include 
_GUICtrlMonthCal_GetMaxSelCount($hWnd)

---  Таблица параметров  ---
$hWnd
	Handle to the control

---  Таблица возвращаемых значений  ---
Успех: INT value that represents the total number of days that can be selected for the control
