
_GUICtrlSlider_GetUnicodeFormat

Retrieves the Unicode character format flag for the control

#Include 
_GUICtrlSlider_GetUnicodeFormat($hWnd)

---  Таблица параметров  ---
$hWnd
	Handle to the control

---  Таблица возвращаемых значений  ---
True: The control is using Unicode characters
False: The control is using ANSI characters

_GUICtrlSlider_SetUnicodeFormat
