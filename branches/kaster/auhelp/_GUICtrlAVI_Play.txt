
_GUICtrlAVI_Play

Plays an AVI clip in an animation control

#Include 
_GUICtrlAVI_Play($hWnd[, $iFrom = 0[, $iTo = -1[, $iRepeat = -1]]])

---  Таблица параметров  ---
$hWnd
	Handle to the control
$iFrom
	[опционально] Zero based index of the frame where playing begins. The value must be less than 65,536. A value
		of 0 means begin with the first frame in the clip.
$iTo
	[опционально] Zero based index of the frame where playing ends. The value must be less than 65,536. A value
		of -1 means end with the last frame in the clip.
$iRepeat
	[опционально] Number of times to replay the AVI clip. A value of -1 means replay the clip indefinitely.

---  Таблица возвращаемых значений  ---
Успех:	True
Ошибка:	False

---  Примечания  ---
The control plays the clip in the background while the thread continues executing
