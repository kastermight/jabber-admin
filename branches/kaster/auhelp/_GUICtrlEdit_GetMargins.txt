
_GUICtrlEdit_GetMargins

Retrieves the widths of the left and right margins

#Include 
_GUICtrlEdit_GetMargins($hWnd)

---  Таблица параметров  ---
$hWnd
	Handle to the control

---  Таблица возвращаемых значений  ---
Успех: Array in the following format:

	[0] - The width of the left margin
	[1] - The width of the right margin
