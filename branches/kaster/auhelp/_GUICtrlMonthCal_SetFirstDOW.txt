
_GUICtrlMonthCal_SetFirstDOW

Sets the first day of the week for a month calendar control

#Include 
_GUICtrlMonthCal_SetFirstDOW($hWnd, $sDay)

---  Таблица параметров  ---
$hWnd
	Handle to the control
$sDay
	In the following format:
		0 or "Monday"
		1 or "Tuesday"
		2 or "Wednesday"
		3 or "Thursday"
		4 or "Friday"
		5 or "Saturday"
		6 or "Sunday"

---  Таблица возвращаемых значений  ---
Успех: The previous first day of the week
Ошибка: -1
