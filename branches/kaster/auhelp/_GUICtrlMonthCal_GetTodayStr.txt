
_GUICtrlMonthCal_GetTodayStr

Retrieves the date information for the date specified as "today" in string format

#Include 
_GUICtrlMonthCal_GetTodayStr($hWnd[, $sFormat = "%02d/%02d/%04d"])

---  Таблица параметров  ---
$hWnd
	Handle to control
$sFormat
	[опционально] StringFormat string used to format the date

---  Таблица возвращаемых значений  ---
Успех: Date in the specified format
