
_WinAPI_SetCursor

Establishes the cursor shape

#Include 
_WinAPI_SetCursor($hCursor)

---  Таблица параметров  ---
$hCursor
	Identifies the cursor

---  Таблица возвращаемых значений  ---
Успех:	The handle of the previous cursor, if there was one.
Ошибка:	0, if there was no previous cursor.
