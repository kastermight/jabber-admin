
_GUICtrlTab_GetCurSel

Determines the currently selected tab

#Include 
_GUICtrlTab_GetCurSel($hWnd)

---  Таблица параметров  ---
$hWnd
	Handle to the control

---  Таблица возвращаемых значений  ---
Успех: The zero based index of the selected tab
Ошибка: -1
