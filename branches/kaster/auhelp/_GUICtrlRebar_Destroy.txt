
_GUICtrlRebar_Destroy

Delete the control

#Include 
_GUICtrlRebar_Destroy(ByRef $hWnd)

---  Таблица параметров  ---
$hWnd
	Handle to the control

---  Таблица возвращаемых значений  ---
Успех:	True, Handle is set to 0
Ошибка:	False

---  Примечания  ---
Restricted to only be used on control created with _GUICtrlRebar_Create
