
_GUICtrlRebar_ShowBand

Shows or hides a given band in a rebar control

#Include 
_GUICtrlRebar_ShowBand($hWnd, $iIndex[, $fShow = True])

---  Таблица параметров  ---
$hWnd
	Handle to rebar control
$iIndex
	Zero-based index of the band
$fShow
	[опционально] indicates if the band should be shown or hidden:
		 True - Show
		False - Hide
