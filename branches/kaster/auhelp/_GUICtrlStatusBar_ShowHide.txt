
_GUICtrlStatusBar_ShowHide

Show/Hide the StatusBar control

#Include 
_GUICtrlStatusBar_ShowHide($hWnd, $iState)

---  Таблица параметров  ---
$hWnd
	Handle to the control
$iState
	State of the StatusBar, can be the following values:
	@SW_SHOW
	@SW_HIDE

---  Таблица возвращаемых значений  ---
True:	The control was previously visible
False:	The control was previously hidden
