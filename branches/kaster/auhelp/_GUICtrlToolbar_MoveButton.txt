
_GUICtrlToolbar_MoveButton

Moves a button from one index to another

#Include 
_GUICtrlToolbar_MoveButton($hWnd, $iOldPos, $iNewPos)

---  Таблица параметров  ---
$hWnd
	Handle to the control
$iOldPos
	Zero based index of the button to be moved
$iNewPos
	Zero based index where the button will be moved

---  Таблица возвращаемых значений  ---
Успех: True
Ошибка: False
