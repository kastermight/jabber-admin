
_GUICtrlTreeView_SetCut

Sets whether the item is drawn as if selected as part of a cut and paste operation

#Include 
_GUICtrlTreeView_SetCut($hWnd, $hItem[, $fFlag = True])

---  Таблица параметров  ---
$hWnd
	Handle to the control
$hItem
	Handle to the item
$fFlag
	[опционально] Flag setting:
		 True - Item is cut
		False - Item is not

---  Таблица возвращаемых значений  ---
Успех:	True
Ошибка:	False
