
_GUICtrlTab_ClickTab

Clicks a tab

#Include 
_GUICtrlTab_ClickTab($hWnd, $iIndex[, $sButton = "left"[, $fMove = False[, $iClicks = 1[, $iSpeed = 1]]]])

---  Таблица параметров  ---
$hWnd
	Handle to control
$iIndex
	Specifies the zero based index of the item
$sButton
	[опционально] Button to click with
$fMove
	[опционально] If True, the mouse will be moved. If False, the mouse does not move.
$iClicks
	[опционально] Number of clicks
$iSpeed
	[опционально] Mouse movement speed
