
_GUICtrlTreeView_DisplayRectEx

Returns the bounding rectangle for a tree item

#Include 
_GUICtrlTreeView_DisplayRectEx($hWnd, $hItem[, $fTextOnly = False])

---  Таблица параметров  ---
$hWnd
	Handle to the control
$hItem
	Handle to the item whose rectangle will be returned
$fTextOnly
	[опционально] If the True, the bounding rectangle includes only the text of the item. Otherwise, it includes
		the entire line that the item occupies.

---  Таблица возвращаемых значений  ---
Успех:	$tagRECT structure that holds the bounding rectangle. The coordinates are relative to the upper left corner of the control.
Ошибка:	Set @error

---  Смотрите также  ---
_GUICtrlTreeView_DisplayRect, $tagRECT
