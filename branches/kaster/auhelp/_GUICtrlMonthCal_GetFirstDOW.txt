
_GUICtrlMonthCal_GetFirstDOW

Retrieves the first day of the week

#Include 
_GUICtrlMonthCal_GetFirstDOW($hWnd)

---  Таблица параметров  ---
$hWnd
	Handle to control

---  Таблица возвращаемых значений  ---
Успех: One of the following values:

	0 - Monday
	1 - Tuesday
	2 - Wednesday
	3 - Thursday
	4 - Friday
	5 - Saturday
	6 - Sunday
