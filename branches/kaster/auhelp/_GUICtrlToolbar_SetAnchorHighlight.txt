
_GUICtrlToolbar_SetAnchorHighlight

Sets the anchor highlight setting

#Include 
_GUICtrlToolbar_SetAnchorHighlight($hWnd, $fAnchor)

---  Таблица параметров  ---
$hWnd
	Handle to the control
$fAnchor
	Anchor highlighting setting:
		 True - Anchor highlighting will be enabled
		False - Anchor highlighting will be disabled

---  Таблица возвращаемых значений  ---
Успех: The previous anchor highlight setting

---  Примечания  ---
Anchor highlighting means that the last highlighted item will remain highlighted until another item is
highlighted. This occurs even if the cursor leaves the toolbar control.
