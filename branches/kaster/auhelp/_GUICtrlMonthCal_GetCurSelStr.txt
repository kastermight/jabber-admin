
_GUICtrlMonthCal_GetCurSelStr

Retrieves the currently selected date in string format

#Include 
_GUICtrlMonthCal_GetCurSelStr($hWnd[, $sFormat = "%02d/%02d/%04d"])

---  Таблица параметров  ---
$hWnd
	Handle to control
$sFormat
	[опционально] StringFormat string used to format the date

---  Таблица возвращаемых значений  ---
Успех: Date in the specified format
