
_GUICtrlButton_Show

Show/Hide button

#Include 
_GUICtrlButton_Show($hWnd[, $fShow = True])

---  Таблица параметров  ---
$hWnd
	Handle to the control
$fShow
	[опционально] One of the following:
		 True - Show button
		False - Hide button

---  Таблица возвращаемых значений  ---
Успех: One of the Following:
		1 - If the button was previously visible
		0 - If the button was previously hidden
