
_GUICtrlComboBoxEx_GetDroppedState

Determines whether the ListBox of a ComboBox is dropped down

#Include 
_GUICtrlComboBoxEx_GetDroppedState($hWnd)

---  Таблица параметров  ---
$hWnd
	Handle to control

---  Таблица возвращаемых значений  ---
True: Is dropped down
False: Not dropped down
