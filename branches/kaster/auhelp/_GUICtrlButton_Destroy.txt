
_GUICtrlButton_Destroy

Delete the Button control

#Include 
_GUICtrlButton_Destroy(ByRef $hWnd)

---  Таблица параметров  ---
$hWnd
	Handle to the control

---  Таблица возвращаемых значений  ---
Успех: True, Handle is set to 0
Ошибка: False

---  Примечания  ---
Restricted to only be used on Edit created with _GUICtrlButton_Create
