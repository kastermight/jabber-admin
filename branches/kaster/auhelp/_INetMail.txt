
_INetMail

Opens default user's mail client with given address, subject, and body.

#include 
_INetMail ( $s_MailTo, $s_MailSubject, $s_MailBody )

---  Таблица параметров  ---
$s_MailTo
	Address for the E-Mail
$s_MailSubject
	Subject for the E-Mail
$s_MailBody
	Body for the E-Mail

---  Таблица возвращаемых значений  ---
Успех:	Process identifier for the E-Mail client
Ошибка:	Returns 0 and sets @error to non-zero.
