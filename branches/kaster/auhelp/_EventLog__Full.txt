
_EventLog__Full

Retrieves whether the event log is full

#Include 
_EventLog__Full($hEventLog)

---  Таблица параметров  ---
$hEventLog
	A handle to the event log

---  Таблица возвращаемых значений  ---
Успех:	True: Event log is full
Ошибка:	False: Event log is not full
