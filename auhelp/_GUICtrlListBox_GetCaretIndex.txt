
_GUICtrlListBox_GetCaretIndex

Return index of item that has the focus rectangle

#Include 
_GUICtrlListBox_GetCaretIndex($hWnd)

---  Таблица параметров  ---
$hWnd
	Handle to control

---  Таблица возвращаемых значений  ---
Успех: Zero based index of the selected item
Ошибка: -1 if nothing is selected

_GUICtrlListBox_SetCaretIndex
