
_GUICtrlSlider_GetThumbRectEx

Retrieves the size and position of the bounding rectangle for the slider

#Include 
_GUICtrlSlider_GetThumbRectEx($hWnd)

---  Таблица параметров  ---
$hWnd
	Handle to the control

---  Таблица возвращаемых значений  ---
Успех: $tagRECT structure that receives the bounding rectangle of the slider in client coordinates
