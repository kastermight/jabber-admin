
_GUICtrlTab_GetItemImage

Retrieves the image index for a tab

#Include 
_GUICtrlTab_GetItemImage($hWnd, $iIndex)

---  Таблица параметров  ---
$hWnd
	Handle to the control
$iIndex
	Zero based item index

---  Таблица возвращаемых значений  ---
Успех: Zero based image index of the tab
