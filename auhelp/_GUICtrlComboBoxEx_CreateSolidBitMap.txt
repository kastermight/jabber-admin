
_GUICtrlComboBoxEx_CreateSolidBitMap

Creates a solid color bitmap

#Include 
_GUICtrlComboBoxEx_CreateSolidBitMap($hWnd, $iColor, $iWidth, $iHeight)

---  Таблица параметров  ---
$hWnd
	Handle to the window where the bitmap will be displayed
$iColor
	The color of the bitmap, stated in RGB
$iWidth
	The width of the bitmap
$iHeight
	The height of the bitmap

---  Таблица возвращаемых значений  ---
Успех: Handle to the bitmap
