
_GUICtrlEdit_PosFromChar

Retrieves the client area coordinates of a specified character in an edit control

#Include 
_GUICtrlEdit_PosFromChar($hWnd, $iIndex)

---  Таблица параметров  ---
$hWnd
	Handle to the control
$iIndex
	The zero-based index of the character

---  Таблица возвращаемых значений  ---
Успех: Array in the following format:

	[0] - The horizontal coordinate
	[1] - The vertical coordinate
