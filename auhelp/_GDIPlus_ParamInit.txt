
_GDIPlus_ParamInit

Initialize an encoder parameter list

#Include 
_GDIPlus_ParamInit($iCount)

---  Таблица параметров  ---
$iCount
	The total number of parameters that the list will contain

---  Таблица возвращаемых значений  ---
Успех:	$tagGDIPPENCODERPARAMS structure
Ошибка:	0

---  Примечания  ---
In order to pass parameters to any of the encoder functions, you must use an encoder parameter list. This
function is used to initialize an encoder parameter list that can then be passed to _GDIPlus_Param add to add the
actual parameters.

---  Смотрите также  ---
_GDIPlus_ParamAdd, $tagGDIPPENCODERPARAMS
