
_GUICtrlListBox_GetListBoxInfo

Retrieve the number of items per column in a specified list box

#Include 
_GUICtrlListBox_GetListBoxInfo($hWnd)

---  Таблица параметров  ---
$hWnd
	Handle to control

---  Таблица возвращаемых значений  ---
Returns the number of items per column

---  Примечания  ---
Minimum OS - Windows XP.
