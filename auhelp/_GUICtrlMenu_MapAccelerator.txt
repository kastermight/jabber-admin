
_GUICtrlMenu_MapAccelerator

Maps a menu accelerator key to it's position in the menu

#Include 
_GUICtrlMenu_MapAccelerator($hMenu, $cAccel)

---  Таблица параметров  ---
$hMenu
	Handle to menu
$cAccel
	Accelerator key

---  Таблица возвращаемых значений  ---
Успех:	The zero based position of the item in the menu
Ошибка:	-1
