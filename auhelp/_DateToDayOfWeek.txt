
_DateToDayOfWeek

Returns the weekdaynumber for a given date.

#Include 
_DateToDayOfWeek($iYear, $iMonth, $iDay)

---  Таблица параметров  ---
$iYear
	A valid year in format YYYY
$iMonth
	A valid month in format MM
$iDay
	A valid day in format DD

---  Таблица возвращаемых значений  ---
Успех:	Returns Day of the Week Range is 1 to 7 where 1=Sunday.
Ошибка:	0 and Set @error
@error:	0 - No error.
	1 - Invalid Input Date
