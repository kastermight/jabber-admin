
_GUICtrlMenu_GetItemSubMenu

Retrieves a the submenu activated by a specified item

#Include 
_GUICtrlMenu_GetItemSubMenu($hMenu, $iItem)

---  Таблица параметров  ---
$hMenu
	Handle of the menu
$iItem
	Zero based position of the menu item

---  Таблица возвращаемых значений  ---
Успех:	A handle to the drop down menu or submenu activated by the menu item
Ошибка:	0

_GUICtrlMenu_SetItemSubMenu, _GUICtrlMenu_RemoveMenu
