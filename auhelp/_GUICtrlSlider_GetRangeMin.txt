
_GUICtrlSlider_GetRangeMin

Retrieves the minimum position for the slider

#Include 
_GUICtrlSlider_GetRangeMin($hWnd)

---  Таблица параметров  ---
$hWnd
	Handle to the control

---  Таблица возвращаемых значений  ---
Успех: The minimum position in the slider's range of minimum to maximum slider positions

_GUICtrlSlider_SetRangeMin, _GUICtrlSlider_GetRange
