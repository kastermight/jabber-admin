
_GUICtrlRebar_GetBandBordersEx

Retrieves the borders of a band. The result of this message can be used to calculate the usable area in a band

#Include 
_GUICtrlRebar_GetBandBordersEx($hWnd, $iIndex)

---  Таблица параметров  ---
$hWnd
	Handle to rebar control
$iIndex
	Zero-based index of the band for which the borders will be retrieved

---  Таблица возвращаемых значений  ---
Returns a $tagRECT structure

_GUICtrlRebar_GetBandBorders, _GUICtrlRebar_GetBandRect, _GUICtrlRebar_GetBandRectEx, $tagRECT
