
_GDIPlus_BrushSetSolidColor

Set the color of a Solid Brush object

#Include 
_GDIPlus_BrushSetSolidColor($hBrush, [$iARGB = 0xFF000000])

---  Таблица параметров  ---
$hBrush
	Handle to a Brush object
$iARGB
	[опционально] Alpha, Red, Green and Blue components of brush

---  Таблица возвращаемых значений  ---
Успех:	True
Ошибка:	False
