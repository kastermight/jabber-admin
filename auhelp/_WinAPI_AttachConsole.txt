
_WinAPI_AttachConsole

Attaches the calling process to the console of the specified process

#Include 
_WinAPI_AttachConsole([$iProcessID = -1])

---  Таблица параметров  ---
$iProcessID
	[опционально] Identifier of the process. Set to -1 to attach to the current process.

---  Таблица возвращаемых значений  ---
Успех:	True
Ошибка:	False
