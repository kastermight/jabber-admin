
_GUICtrlListView_SetItemCut

Sets whether the item is marked for a cut-and-paste operation

#Include 
_GUICtrlListView_SetItemCut($hWnd, $iIndex[, $fEnabled = True])

---  Таблица параметров  ---
$hWnd
	Handle to the control
$iIndex
	Zero based index of the item
$fEnabled
	[опционально] If True the item state is set, otherwise it is not set.

---  Таблица возвращаемых значений  ---
Успех:	True
Ошибка:	False
