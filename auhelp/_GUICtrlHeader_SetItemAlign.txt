
_GUICtrlHeader_SetItemAlign

Sets the item text alignment

#Include 
_GUICtrlHeader_SetItemAlign($hWnd, $iIndex, $iAlign)

---  Таблица параметров  ---
$hWnd
	Handle to control
$iIndex
	Zero based item index
$iAlign
	Text alignment:
		0 - Text is left-aligned
		1 - Text is right-aligned
		2 - Text is centered

---  Таблица возвращаемых значений  ---
Успех: True
Ошибка: False
