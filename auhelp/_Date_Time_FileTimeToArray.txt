
_Date_Time_FileTimeToArray

Decode a file time to an array

#Include 
_Date_Time_FileTimeToArray(ByRef $tFileTime)

---  Таблица параметров  ---
$tFileTime
	$tagFILETIME structure

---  Таблица возвращаемых значений  ---
Returns an Array with the following format:
		[0] - Month
		[1] - Day
		[2] - Year
		[3] - Hour
		[4] - Minutes
		[5] - Seconds
		[6] - Milliseconds
		[7] - Day of week

---  Смотрите также  ---
$tagFILETIME
