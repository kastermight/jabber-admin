
_GUICtrlMonthCal_GetSelRangeMaxStr

Retrieves the upper date range currently selected by the user in string form

#Include 
_GUICtrlMonthCal_GetSelRangeMaxStr($hWnd[, $sFormat = "%02d/%02d/%04d"])

---  Таблица параметров  ---
$hWnd
	Handle to control
$sFormat
	[опционально] StringFormat string used to format the date

---  Таблица возвращаемых значений  ---
Успех: Date in the specified format
