
_GUICtrlMenu_FindItem

Retrieves a menu item based on it's text

#Include 
_GUICtrlMenu_FindItem($hMenu, $sText[, $fInStr = False[, $iStart = 0]])

---  Таблица параметров  ---
$hMenu
	Menu handle
$sText
	Text to search for
$fInStr
	[опционально] If True, the text can be anywhere in the item's text.
$iStart
	[опционально] Item to start searching from

---  Таблица возвращаемых значений  ---
Успех:	The zero based index of the first item that contains the text
Ошибка:	-1

---  Примечания  ---
The search is case insensitive
