
_GUICtrlComboBoxEx_DeleteString

Removes an item from a ComboBoxEx control

#Include 
_GUICtrlComboBoxEx_DeleteString($hWnd, $iIndex)

---  Таблица параметров  ---
$hWnd
	Handle to the control
$iIndex
	Zero based index of the Item to delete

---  Таблица возвращаемых значений  ---
Успех: Number of items remaining in the control
