
_GUICtrlDTP_SetMCFont

Sets the month calendar font

#Include 
_GUICtrlDTP_SetMCFont($hWnd, $hFont[, $fRedraw = True])

---  Таблица параметров  ---
$hWnd
	Handle to the control
$hFont
	Handle to the font that will be set
$fRedraw
	[опционально] Specifies whether the control should be redrawn immediately
