
_GDIPlus_ImageGetHeight

Get the image height

#Include 
_GDIPlus_ImageGetHeight($hImage)

---  Таблица параметров  ---
$hImage
	Handle to an image object

---  Таблица возвращаемых значений  ---
Успех:	Image height, in pixels
Ошибка:	-1 and @error is set
