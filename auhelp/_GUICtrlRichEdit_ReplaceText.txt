
_GUICtrlRichEdit_ReplaceText

Replaces selected text

#Include 
_GUICtrlRichEdit_ReplaceText($hWnd, $sText[, $fCanUndo = True])

---  Таблица параметров  ---
$hWnd
	Handle to the control
$sText
	Replacement text
$fCanUndo
	[опционально] Can operation can be undone? True (Default) or False

---  Таблица возвращаемых значений  ---
Успех:	True
Ошибка:	False and sets @error
@error:	101 - $hWnd is not a handle
	103 - $fCanUndo must be True or False
	-1 - no text is selected
