
_GUICtrlToolbar_SetStyleCustomErase

Sets whether the control generates NM_CUSTOMDRAW notification messages

#Include 
_GUICtrlToolbar_SetStyleCustomErase($hWnd[, $fState = True])

---  Таблица параметров  ---
$hWnd
	Handle to the control
$fState
	[опционально] True to set, False to unset

---  Таблица возвращаемых значений  ---
Успех:	True
Ошибка:	False
