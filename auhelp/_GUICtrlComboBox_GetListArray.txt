
_GUICtrlComboBox_GetListArray

Retrieves all items from the list portion of a ComboBox control

#Include 
_GUICtrlComboBox_GetListArray($hWnd)

---  Таблица параметров  ---
$hWnd
	Handle to control

---  Таблица возвращаемых значений  ---
Успех: Array in the following format:
		[0] - Number of items
		[1] - Item 1
		[2] - Item 2
		[n] - Item n
