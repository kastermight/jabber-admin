
_GUICtrlTab_SetItemSize

Sets the width and height of tabs in a fixed width or owner drawn control

#Include 
_GUICtrlTab_SetItemSize($hWnd, $iWidth, $iHeight)

---  Таблица параметров  ---
$hWnd
	Handle to the control
$iWidth
	New width, in pixels
$iHeight
	New height, in pixels

---  Таблица возвращаемых значений  ---
Успех: Returns the old width and height. The width is in the low order word of the return value, and
		the height is in the high-order word.
