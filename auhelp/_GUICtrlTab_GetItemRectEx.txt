
_GUICtrlTab_GetItemRectEx

Retrieves the bounding rectangle for a tab

#Include 
_GUICtrlTab_GetItemRectEx($hWnd, $iIndex)

---  Таблица параметров  ---
$hWnd
	Handle to the control
$iIndex
	Zero based item item

---  Таблица возвращаемых значений  ---
Успех: $tagRECT structure that receives the rectangle of the tab, in viewport coordinates

---  Смотрите также  ---
_GUICtrlTab_GetItemRect, $tagRECT
