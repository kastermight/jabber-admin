
_GUIToolTip_TrackActivate

Activates or deactivates a tracking ToolTip

#Include 
_GUIToolTip_TrackActivate($hWnd[, $fActivate = True[, $hTool = 0[, $iID = 0]]])

---  Таблица параметров  ---
$hWnd
	Handle to control
$fActivate
	[опционально] True to activate, False to deactivate
$hTool
	[опционально] Handle to the window that contains the tool
$iID
	[опционально] Application-defined identifier of the tool
