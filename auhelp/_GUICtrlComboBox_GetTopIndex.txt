
_GUICtrlComboBox_GetTopIndex

Retrieve the zero-based index of the first visible item in the ListBox portion of a ComboBox

#Include 
_GUICtrlComboBox_GetTopIndex($hWnd)

---  Таблица параметров  ---
$hWnd
	Handle to control

---  Таблица возвращаемых значений  ---
Успех: The index of the first visible item in the ListBox of the ComboBox
Ошибка: -1
