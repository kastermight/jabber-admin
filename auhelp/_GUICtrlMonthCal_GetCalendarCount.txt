
_GUICtrlMonthCal_GetCalendarCount

Gets the number of calendars currently displayed in the calendar control

#Include 
_GUICtrlMonthCal_GetCalendarCount($hWnd)

---  Таблица параметров  ---
$hWnd
	Handle to control

---  Таблица возвращаемых значений  ---
Успех: Number of calendars currently displayed in the calendar control. The maximum number of allowed calendars is 12

---  Примечания  ---
Minimum Operating Systems: Windows Vista
