
_DateDaysInMonth

Returns the number of days in a month, based on the specified month and year.

#Include 
_DateDaysInMonth($iYear, $iMonthNum)

---  Таблица параметров  ---
$iYear
	4-digit year.
$iMonthNum
	Month number (1 = January, 12 = December).

---  Таблица возвращаемых значений  ---
Успех:	Returns the number of days in the month.
Ошибка:	Returns 0 and set @error
@error:	0 - No error.
	1 - Invalid month number or year.
