
_GUICtrlHeader_SetBitmapMargin

Sets the width of the margin, specified in pixels, of a bitmap

#Include 
_GUICtrlHeader_SetBitmapMargin($hWnd, $iWidth)

---  Таблица параметров  ---
$hWnd
	Handle to control
$iWidth
	Width, specified in pixels, of the bitmap margin

---  Таблица возвращаемых значений  ---
Успех: The previous bitmap margin width
Ошибка: 0
