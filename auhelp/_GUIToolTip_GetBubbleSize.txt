
_GUIToolTip_GetBubbleSize

Returns the width and height of the control

#Include 
_GUIToolTip_GetBubbleSize($hWnd, $hTool, $iID[, $iFlags = 0x000000A0])

---  Таблица параметров  ---
$hWnd
	Handle to control
$hTool
	Handle to the window that contains the tool
$iID
	Identifier of the tool
$iFlags
	[опционально] Flags that control the ToolTip display

---  Таблица возвращаемых значений  ---
Успех: Returns the width of the ToolTip in the low word and the height in the high word

---  Смотрите также  ---
_GUIToolTip_GetBubbleHeight, _GUIToolTip_GetBubbleWidth
