
_GUICtrlToolbar_LoadImages

Loads system defined button images into a toolbar control's image list

#Include 
_GUICtrlToolbar_LoadImages($hWnd, $iBitMapID)

---  Таблица параметров  ---
$hWnd
	Handle to the control
$iBitMapID
	Identifier of a system-defined button image list. Can be set to one of the following values:
		$IDB_HIST_LARGE_COLOR
		$IDB_HIST_SMALL_COLOR
		$IDB_STD_LARGE_COLOR
		$IDB_STD_SMALL_COLOR
		$IDB_VIEW_LARGE_COLOR
		$IDB_VIEW_SMALL_COLOR
		$IDB_HIST_NORMAL
		$IDB_HIST_HOT
		$IDB_HIST_DISABLED
		$IDB_HIST_PRESSED

---  Таблица возвращаемых значений  ---
Успех: The count of images in the image list, not including the ones just added
Ошибка: 0
