
_GUICtrlToolbar_GetStyleWrapable

Indicates that the control is wrapable

#Include 
_GUICtrlToolbar_GetStyleWrapable($hWnd)

---  Таблица параметров  ---
$hWnd
	Handle to the control

---  Таблица возвращаемых значений  ---
True: Style is present
False: Style is not present
