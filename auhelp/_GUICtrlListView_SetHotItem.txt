
_GUICtrlListView_SetHotItem

Sets the hot item

#Include 
_GUICtrlListView_SetHotItem($hWnd, $iIndex)

---  Таблица параметров  ---
$hWnd
	Handle to the control
$iIndex
	Zero-based index of the item to be set as the hot item

---  Таблица возвращаемых значений  ---
Успех:	Index of the item that was previously hot
Ошибка:	-1
