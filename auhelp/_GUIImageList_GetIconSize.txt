
_GUIImageList_GetIconSize

Retrieves the dimensions of images in an image list

#Include 
_GUIImageList_GetIconSize($hWnd)

---  Таблица параметров  ---
$hWnd
	Дескриптор элемента

---  Таблица возвращаемых значений  ---
Returns an Array with the following format:

	[0] - Width, in pixels, of each image
	[1] - Height, in pixels, of each image

_GUIImageList_GetIconHeight, _GUIImageList_GetIconSizeEx, _GUIImageList_GetIconWidth, _GUIImageList_SetIconSize
