
_GUICtrlTreeView_AddChildFirst

Adds a new item

#Include 
_GUICtrlTreeView_AddChildFirst($hWnd, $hParent, $sText[, $iImage = -1[, $iSelImage = -1]])

---  Таблица параметров  ---
$hWnd
	Handle to the control
$hParent
	Parent item
$sText
	Text of the item
$iImage
	[опционально] Zero based index of the item's icon in the control's image list
$iSelImage
	[опционально] Zero based index of the item's icon in the control's image list

---  Таблица возвращаемых значений  ---
Успех:	The handle to the new item
Ошибка:	0

---  Примечания  ---
The item is added as the first child of $hParent. Items that appear after the added item are moved down.
