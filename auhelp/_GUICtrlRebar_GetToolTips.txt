
_GUICtrlRebar_GetToolTips

Retrieves the handle to any ToolTip control associated with the rebar control

#Include 
_GUICtrlRebar_GetToolTips($hWnd)

---  Таблица параметров  ---
$hWnd
	Handle to rebar control

---  Таблица возвращаемых значений  ---
Успех:	The handle to the ToolTip control associated with the rebar control
Ошибка:	0 if no ToolTip control is associated with the rebar control

---  Смотрите также  ---
_GUICtrlRebar_SetToolTips
