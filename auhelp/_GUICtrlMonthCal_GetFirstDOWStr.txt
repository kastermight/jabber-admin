
_GUICtrlMonthCal_GetFirstDOWStr

Retrieves the first day of the week as a string

#Include 
_GUICtrlMonthCal_GetFirstDOWStr($hWnd)

---  Таблица параметров  ---
$hWnd
	Handle to control

---  Таблица возвращаемых значений  ---
Успех: One of the following values:

	Monday
	Tuesday
	Wednesday
	Thursday
	Friday
	Saturday
	Sunday
