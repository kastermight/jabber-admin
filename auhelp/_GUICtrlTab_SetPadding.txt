
_GUICtrlTab_SetPadding

Sets the amount of space around each tab's icon and label

#Include 
_GUICtrlTab_SetPadding($hWnd, $iHorz, $iVert)

---  Таблица параметров  ---
$hWnd
	Handle to the control
$iHorz
	Amount of horizontal padding, in pixels
$iVert
	Amount of vertical padding, in pixels
