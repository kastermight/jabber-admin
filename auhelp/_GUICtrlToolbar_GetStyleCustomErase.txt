
_GUICtrlToolbar_GetStyleCustomErase

Indicates that the control generates NM_CUSTOMDRAW notification messages

#Include 
_GUICtrlToolbar_GetStyleCustomErase($hWnd)

---  Таблица параметров  ---
$hWnd
	Handle to the control

---  Таблица возвращаемых значений  ---
True: Style is present
False: Style is not present
